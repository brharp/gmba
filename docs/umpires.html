Level System

GMBA LevelUmpire ExpectationAssociation Expectations
1
  • Late season T Bal, Rookie Ball House League
  • 3-6 games per month
  • Basic Positioning (P1, P2, P3 and P4)
  • Rules knowledge (basic understanding of the game of baseball)
  • How and when to move to the next position (90 degree angle, Pivot, getting out from behind home plate)
  • Basic Mechanics (Out/Safe, Ball/Strike)
  • Flyball coverage areas
  • Fair/Foul Responsibilities and definitions
  • Successful evaluation needed for advancement
2
  • Rookie Ball House League & Rep and Mosquito House League
  • 1-2 games a week
  • All previous requirements
  • Rules knowledge (Infield Fly, overthrow, live/dead ball)
  • Proper Timing (allowing the play to happen)
  • Proper Voice (Loud, Clear, Confident)
  • Successful evaluation needed for advancement
3
  • Rookie Ball Rep, Mosquito House League & Rep, Peewee House League
  • 1-3 games a week  
  • All previous requirements
  • Rules knowledge (Contact, reentry, balk)
  • First to Third rotations
  • Advanced flyball mechanics
  • Signals
  • Successful evaluation needed for advancement
4
  • Mosquito House League & Rep, Peewee House League & Rep, Bantam House League
  • 1-3 games per week
  • All previous requirements
  • Rules knowledge (Interference, Obstruction, Scoring a run)
  • Mechanics review
  • Game management techniques
  • Successful evaluation needed for advancement
5
  • Peewee Rep, Bantam House League & Rep
  • 1-4 games per week
  • All previous requirements
  • Rules knowledge (Batting out of order, EP, DH)
  • Situational management
  • Plays at the plate
  • Successful evaluation needed for advancement
6
  • Peewee Rep, Bantam House League & Rep, Midget House League, Midget tier 2 (special circumstances only)
  • 1-4 games a week
  • All previous requirements
  • Tag up responsibilities
  • Mastery of skill
  • Understanding of the three umpire system
  • Successful evaluation at Tournament or Championship Weekend (needed for advancement)
7
  • Bantam House League & Rep, Midget House League & Rep.
  • 1-3 games a week
  • All previous requirements
  • Three umpire system

Equipment

As covered in the level 1 and 2 clinics umpires will need the following; indicator, ball bag, plate brush, jersey, plain black hat, protective equipment, grey pants, black shoes, black belt and black socks.

Grey pants (dress type, that shin guards can fit underneath), black (or mostly black) shoes - can be baseball cleats, a black belt and black socks are not available for purchase through GMBA.
